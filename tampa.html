City of Tampa

Tampa home to the Tampa bay buccaneers and Tampa bay Rays, and is considered the third largest city in Florida. Tampa was reincorporated as a city after the civil war in 1887. The city has a lot of history and many historical areas to visit. It has some beautiful beaches and flies under the radar for many tourist and locals. It has a population of 408,438.
