The state of Florida is known as the sunshine state. Florida is one of the fastest growing states in the United States of America with a population of 21.48 million people currently residing there. It's also a major tourist destination for many people around the world. Florida has a vast ecosystem of animals and different things to do from the beach to the amusement parks. On March 3, 1845, Florida was added as the 27th state of the United State of America. The capital is Tallahassee.
